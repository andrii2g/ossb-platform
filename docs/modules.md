# Modules

## Billing

Billing will manage charges, invoice cycles, balances, and debt visibility for OSBB operations. It is expected to become a core service area and a dependency for reporting and notifications.

## Payments

Payments will focus on reconciliation and payment intake workflows. It will connect incoming transactions to invoices, balances, and audit-friendly records.

## Residents

Residents will provide a structured registry of units, owners, tenants, and related administrative metadata. This module will support downstream billing, communication, and governance scenarios.

## Notifications

Notifications will centralize outbound communication logic for reminders, billing notices, announcements, and operational alerts. Delivery channels may expand over time.

## Maintenance

Maintenance will cover issue intake, service requests, assignment flows, and resolution tracking. It is intended to support both residents and building administration processes.

## Document Storage

Document storage will provide a place for invoices, statements, contracts, protocols, and other operational files. It should align with access control and retention requirements later.

## Voting and Governance

Voting and governance will support meeting workflows, agenda distribution, resident participation, and decision records. This area is planned for a later maturity stage of the platform.

## Invoice Generator

Invoice Generator is the first planned tool in the repository. It will initially focus on practical invoice document generation and later integrate with billing and document storage workflows.
