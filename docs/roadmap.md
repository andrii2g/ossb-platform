# Roadmap

## Phase 1: Bootstrap Repository and Invoice Generator

- Establish the monorepo structure
- Define documentation and developer conventions
- Add the initial Invoice Generator tool scaffold
- Prepare shared folders for future services and libraries

## Phase 2: Billing and Debt Tracking

- Introduce billing domain services
- Model charges, billing periods, and account balances
- Support debt tracking and reporting workflows

## Phase 3: Payment Reconciliation

- Add payment import and reconciliation capabilities
- Prepare integration boundaries for bank statements and provider data
- Connect payment results to billing balances

## Phase 4: Residents Registry and Notifications

- Introduce resident and unit registry capabilities
- Model communication preferences and administrative contacts
- Add notification workflows for billing and operational events

## Phase 5: Maintenance, Helpdesk, and Analytics

- Add maintenance request handling and task coordination
- Introduce service and issue history tracking
- Expand into reporting, dashboards, and operational analytics
